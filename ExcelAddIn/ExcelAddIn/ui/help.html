FinLite AI Assistant Add-In Help

Introduction

FinLite AI Assistant is an VSTO-based Excel add-in that provides financial analysis and intelligent question-answering capabilities. Built with cutting-edge Large Language Model (LLM) technology, it transforms your Excel experience by enabling natural language interaction with your financial data, comprehensive data analysis, and intelligent insights generation.

Using the Add-In

Core Features

  • Cell Selection: Select any range of cells in Excel to provide focused context for your queries. The selected data appears in the "Selection preview" panel, allowing the add-in to provide targeted analysis of specific datasets. Leave cells unselected to analyze entire worksheets or workbooks.
  • Natural Language Queries: Ask questions about your data in plain English. Type your question in the prompt box and press Enter to send, or use Ctrl+Enter to add new lines for complex multi-line questions. FinLite understands financial terminology and can provide summarization, trend analysis, and data interpretation.
  • Voice Input: Click the microphone button to start recording your voice. Speak your question naturally and click again to stop recording. FinLite will automatically convert speech to text and insert it into the prompt area.
  • View Chat History: The latest ten conversation sessions are automatically saved and organized chronologically. Access previous discussions by clicking the folder icon on the top to reload past conversations.
  • Multiple Response Modes: Choose between Concise (quick insights), Detailed (comprehensive analysis), or Formula (financial formula explanation) response modes to match your specific workflow needs.

Interface Controls

  • Response Mode Dropdown: Select Concise, Detailed, or Formula mode to control AI response type and depth.
  • New Chat (): Start a fresh conversation, clearing current chat from view while preserving history.
  • History (): View and reload previous chat sessions. Press Esc to close.
  • About (): Display add-in information and development credits. Press Esc to close.
  • Help (): Open this help documentation.
  • Microphone (): Click and hold to record voice questions, release to send.
  • Send (): Submit your typed question for AI analysis.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Enter: Send question
  • Ctrl+Enter: Add new line in question box
  • Ctrl+M: Start/stop voice recording
  • Ctrl+Shift+↑/↓/←/→: Extend Excel selection in direction
  • Esc: Close any open popover (e.g. About)

Advanced Tips

  • Data Context: For best results, ensure your Excel data has clear headers and is well-organized. FinLite can better understand and analyze structured data.
  • Question Types: Ask about trends, comparisons, calculations, forecasts, or data relationships. Examples: "What's the growth trend?", "Compare Q1 vs Q2 revenue", "Calculate the moving average".
  • Insufficient evidence message: If you see "Insufficient evidence. Please provide more context or initialize data first.", it means no relevant information was detected. Try selecting the relevant cells in Excel to provide context when querying.
